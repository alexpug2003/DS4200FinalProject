Age Distribution by Diagnosis (Dropdown Country Filter)

This interactive violin plot displays how age is distributed among individuals with or without Alzheimer’s, based on the selected country (USA, Canada, or Mexico).

Interpretation

The country dropdown lets you view the distribution of ages across diagnosed vs. non-diagnosed individuals in different countries. These distributions help visualize how Alzheimer’s manifests across age ranges in different populations.
